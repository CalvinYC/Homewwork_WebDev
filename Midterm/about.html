About Us

DeCign is a web system that designed to discover designers’ ideas, mainly using images and shorter articles to share creativity with every designers. The site was founded by Calvin Chen in March of 2018, with the goal to "Share the Designs".

After putting into use, DeCign is keeping its purpose to construct a "World-Wide Design Ecosphere", providing designers a platform to share, learn, show and communicate.

DeCign is a leading online platform to showcase & discover creative work, to remove the barriers between talent & opportunity. The creative world updates their work in one place to broadcast it widely and efficiently. Companies explore the work and access talent on a global scale.

In all, we keeps focusing on building bridges between designers and designers, since we believe gathering togather to share ideas and information, designers can get better working environment.

In the future, DeCign is going to set up the relationship between designers and businesses, so as to help talented designers to get better working opportunity. We are still step by step making creativities to change the world.

We appreciate every user who is interested in and loves DeCign. We wish everyone who joins us can enjoy the delights of design and share.
